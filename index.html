About Us

Building community, inspiring people

Welcome to Sign, a community of enthusiasts united by a passion for innovation and aspiration for development. We created Orange Dynasty to inspire, educate and help each member to reach their potential.

Our mission is to unite people with common interests, create a comfortable environment for communication and knowledge sharing. We believe that success is the result of persistence, support and teamwork.

Join us and let's build a community together where everyone will find their place!

How to farm Sign

Please forget everything you know about web3 community culture and try to look at it from a new perspective.

Set your expectations

Don't think "what can I do for Sign?", think "how can Sign best help me?" Decide in what direction you want to develop your platform (graphics, threads, Spaces, etc.) and go for it. Our Orange Dynasty will support you and highlight your achievements.

Social positioning

Sign is still in its very early stages. The fact that you can brag about seeing Sign before anyone else can give you some leverage as an "alpha caller".

Be the best version of yourself

Create the kind of content you want to be known for, striving to perfect your craft and grow your platform. All great creators start with this mindset — and at Sign, you’ll have a strong support system behind you.

SBT Types

SBTs are honorary badges for contributions to the Orange Dynasty. There are 4 types, distributed in rounds, each eligible for a $Sign airdrop during TGE. All participants have equal chances.

Support Warrior

For those who actively support the members of the Orange Dynasty, provide genuine feedback on their posts, and participate in @Sign activities on X.

Orange in the Veins

For those who regularly participate in community events hosted by moderator @0xzoe_im, attend Twitter Spaces, and actively engage in our group chat on Telegram.

Outstanding Content Creation

For those who constantly strive to improve their content creation skills by experimenting with different formats and styles.

Serious Builders

For those who go beyond the ordinary and put maximum effort into their work in a unique way.
